About Me

I am the Founder and CEO of Pokee AI, where we're developing the next generation of interactive, personalized, and scalable AI agents using reinforcement learning. Our mission is to create AI systems that can adapt to human needs and preferences while maintaining safety and reliability.

Prior to founding Pokee AI, I spent 7 years at Meta AI as the Head of the Applied Reinforcement Learning Group. During my tenure, I led the development of RL solutions for recommendation systems and advertising, playing a key role in scaling AI technologies across Meta's family of apps.

I hold a Ph.D. in Reinforcement Learning from Stanford University, where my research focused on developing algorithms for safe and adaptive AI systems. My academic background and industry experience have given me a unique perspective on how to translate cutting-edge AI research into practical applications that can benefit millions of users.

My research interests include reinforcement learning, multi-agent systems, recommendation algorithms, and safe AI deployment. I'm particularly passionate about creating AI systems that can learn from human feedback and adapt to changing environments while maintaining safety guarantees.
